Frequently Asked Questions

How can I book an ambulance?

You can use the Emergency Booking section to book an ambulance quickly. Simply fill out the required details and submit your request.

Is HealthBuzz free to use?

Yes, most of our services are free, but some advanced features like premium health monitoring may require a subscription.

How accurate is the health assessment tool?

Our health assessment tool uses AI-based algorithms and is designed to provide general recommendations. However, it is not a substitute for professional medical advice.

Can I track my health data over time?

Yes, with our Health Monitoring feature, you can track your vital signs and progress over time to stay informed about your health.

How do I find nearby hospitals?

Use our Facility Locator tool to find nearby hospitals and clinics. It provides real-time availability information for your convenience.

How secure is my personal information?

Your privacy is our priority. All personal data is encrypted and stored securely in compliance with industry standards.
